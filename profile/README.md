## 🚀 What It Is
Topaz Denoise AI for Windows is a Topaz Labs denoise app that applies deep-learning models to strip noise from your photographs without washing away detail. Where classic filters trade sharpness for smoothness, this Topaz AI denoise tool recognizes what is genuine texture and what is sensor noise, then rebuilds a clean result. It shines with low-light captures and high-ISO frames, giving you images you can actually keep and print.

> Noise reduction should never cost you detail. Topaz Denoise AI is designed to remove grain while your subject stays crisp and true to life.

## ✨ Key Features
- 🧠 **AI noise reduction** — A trained model removes luminance and color noise for results that look natural, not smeared.
- 🔍 **Detail preservation** — Keeps edges, textures, and fine structures intact while grain disappears.
- 🌙 **Low-light rescue** — Recovers usable photos from dim scenes and extreme ISO settings.
- 📁 **Batch mode** — Process many photos in one pass for faster turnaround on big shoots.
- 🎚️ **Adjustable strength** — Dial the amount of noise reduction up or down to match each image.

## ❓ Questions
**Is Topaz Denoise AI free?**
Topaz Denoise AI is a paid app from Topaz Labs with a free trial, so you can try the AI denoise engine on your own photos first. Topaz Labs has also combined denoise, sharpen, and upscale into a single Topaz Photo AI app, while Denoise AI stays focused on noise reduction.

**Which versions of Windows are supported?**
It supports modern 64-bit Windows releases such as Windows 10 and Windows 11. See the official Topaz Labs website for the exact current requirements.

**Does it clean up video noise?**
No — Topaz Denoise AI works on still photos. For footage, Topaz Video AI is the separate application that handles video denoising.
